What is URIX?

URIX aims to have wider hardware compatability and offer the stability of openSuSe. All software products are carefully picked so that they don't waste your resources or take hardisk space. URIX is a Linux on chip, meaning that it runs from USB without the need of any installation. It is also the sucessor of NetSecL OS also know as ISlack. The distribution has several sub projects:

URIX OS - General Purpose OS

URIX VM - Virtualization

How to use URIX?

It easy, burn it on a usb drive with Win32DISKImager or dd under Linux. Boot. Use linux as password for root and tux user.
